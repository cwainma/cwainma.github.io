Beard and Body

Opportunity

A hypothetical natural, fair trade soap company has developed a line of men’s hygiene and grooming products. This new product line needed a name, identity and package design that would effectively target young men and middle-aged women purchasers.

Action

Special consideration was needed to overcome the femine design surrounding most of the natural hygiene product offerings. Studies have shown that men tend to favour products that fit gender expectations. The name Beard and Body states that this product line is made exclusively for men and isn’t the secondary line to a female product. The dark colours and ergonomic shapes fit the gender profile and resemble some of the key elements found in the non-natural products offerings making it an easy transition product.
