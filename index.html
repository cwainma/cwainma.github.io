About Me

I became interested in graphic design while working as an account consultant selling advertising. When the opportunity arose, I pursued design. Through all the typography, the software applications and marketing plans, one thing has always been clear— it’s all about communicating with the audience and that is something I’ve been working at for quite some time. Now a recent graduate from Kwantlen Polytechnic University, I’m armed with a Bachelors in Graphic Design for Marketing and I’m looking to work with an amazing team.
